Magic Cake
Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.

Ingredients
2 eggs
1 pund of sugar
3 sticks of butter
Directions
Mix eggs, sugar, and buter in a large bowl.
Spread into a non-stick dish
Bake at 350 degree for 1 hour
Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
Eat and enjoy!
